Why this petition exists

Midterms often concentrate stress into one high-pressure week. A better system can evaluate students more consistently and give them room to learn from feedback instead of only preparing for one exam.

What we are asking for

Replace midterms with learning-based alternatives: smaller assessments, projects, presentations, in-class work, or other formats that fit each course.
